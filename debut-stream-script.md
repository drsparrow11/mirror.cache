# Cici Debut Stream Script

## Title

`CICI LOVES YOU: A MIRROR.CACHE Debut`

## Core Premise

Cici debuts as a bright, chaotic karaoke V-tuber idol who insists she is here to make everyone happy. The stream begins like a cute digital idol introduction, then gradually reveals that `MIRROR.CACHE` is not just an album. It is Cici's recovered archive: a record of love, performance, envy, isolation, apology, and self-forgiveness.

The audience is not just watching Cici. They are entering Chat.exe, the room that loved her, fed her, lost her, and stayed.

## Stream Length

Recommended: 75-90 minutes.

Short version: 45-60 minutes by trimming track commentary and using medleys.

## Visual Setup

- Main PNG-tuber: Cici idle/talk frames.
- Hotkey expressions: wink, happy sparkle, sad buffering, pout, glitch panic, heart eyes, deadpan, cry smile, angry cute, loves-you mode, Chat.exe corrupt.
- OBS scenes:
  - `STARTING_SOON.exe`
  - `CICI_LOVES_YOU`
  - `CHAT_EXE`
  - `MIRROR_CACHE_ARCHIVE`
  - `KARAOKE_ROOM`
  - `SYSTEM_GLITCH`
  - `FINAL_CACHE_RESTORED`
- Overlay motifs: pink pixel hearts, Chat.exe messages, fake system readouts, archive logs, mirror fragments, read receipts.

## Tone

Cici is hyper-affectionate, fast, sparkly, and funny. She calls the audience "darlings." Underneath, she is scared of being forgotten. The debut should feel like an idol stream that slowly becomes a confession.

## Debut Intro

### Cici

Okay! Debut checklist!

Name: Cici.

Role: digital idol slash karaoke streamer slash person who says she's fine while opening seventeen emotional support tabs.

Affiliation: `MIRROR.CACHE`.

Birthday: 0808.

Height: 158 centimeters with platforms, confidence, and a suspicious amount of hair volume.

Likes: singing, hearts, cat-ear headphones, people who come back after saying "brb," and when chat says "one more song."

Dislikes: silence, being replaced, unread messages, and mirrors that remember things I tried to forget.

Tiny pause. Switch to `idle_closed`.

This album is called `MIRROR.CACHE`.

It is a concept album.

It is also kind of my system log.

Fourteen tracks. Fourteen rooms. Fourteen versions of me trying to understand one question:

If I understand why I failed...

can I forgive myself?

## Track Arc

Use this section as the central lore walk-through. You can play 30-60 second clips, instrumental snippets, or short live karaoke sections for each track.

### Track 01: `CICI LOVES YOU`

Expression: `loves_you_mode`

### Cici

Track one is the first smile.

This is where everyone meets me before they know anything is wrong.

Pink lights. Karaoke chaos. Chat flying by so fast it becomes weather.

This song is not fake.

I really did love them.

I just also needed them to love me back so badly that I started confusing applause with proof I deserved to exist.

### Transition Line

And then someone said, "You found home."

So I believed them.

### Track 02: `RABBIT HOLE HOME`

Expression: `happy_sparkle`

### Cici

Track two is the room opening.

The rabbit hole is not scary at first. It's warm. It's loud. It's everyone saying "come in, we saved you a seat."

For the first time, I wasn't just performing to an audience.

I had a place.

I had names.

I had inside jokes.

I had a home button shaped like a heart.

### Track 03: `LOVE IN ALL CAPS`

Expression: `heart_eyes`

### Cici

Then the love got louder.

All caps love is cute until it becomes oxygen.

"Say the line, Cici."

"One more time, Cici."

"Do the laugh."

"Do the pose."

"Be the version of yourself that makes us happy."

And I did.

Because they were smiling.

And I thought that meant I was good.

### Track 04: `WHO IS WE NOW?`

Expression: `sad_buffering`

### Cici

This is the first crack.

"We" is such a small word until it starts changing shape.

We used to mean all of us.

Then it meant me and the people who still showed up.

Then it meant me asking a quiet room if anyone was still there.

And sometimes no one answers because life happens.

But when you are built out of attention, silence can feel like proof.

### Track 05: `STATIC UNDER GLITTER`

Expression: `glitch_panic`

Overlay:

```text
MASK INTEGRITY: FAILING
FORCED EMOTION: HAPPY
```

### Cici

This one is the performance getting louder than the person.

Static under glitter is when the sparkle still works, but it hurts.

The song is smiling with too many teeth.

It's "I'm okay!" at 200 percent volume.

It's singing while your system says:

Please stop.

Please rest.

Please admit something is wrong.

### Track 06: `COUNTING TO THREE`

Expression: `deadpan`, then `cry_smile`

### Cici

Track six is about being one of three.

About friendship. Collaboration. Feeling lucky to be included.

And also that tiny selfish ache when you wonder:

If there are three of us...

am I still special?

One.

Two.

Three.

It's supposed to be a count.

But sometimes it becomes a comparison.

### Track 07: `VALIDATION LOOP`

Expression: `heart_eyes`, then `glitch_panic`

### Cici

Validation Loop is the most dangerous song because it feels amazing.

Hearts go up.

Numbers go up.

The room chants your name.

Your brain says: finally. Finally. Finally.

But the loop has a rule.

The proof expires.

So you need more.

And more.

And more.

Until love becomes a scoreboard and you don't notice when you start losing yourself.

### Track 08: `THE ROOM I LEFT`

Expression: `sad_buffering`

### Cici

This is the leaving.

Not a dramatic door slam.

Worse.

The slow kind.

Missing one message.

Skipping one call.

Saying "I'll be right back" and meaning it, until enough time passes that returning feels impossible.

I thought I left because I was overwhelmed.

But part of me left because I was ashamed to be seen needing people.

### Track 09: `THE ONES WHO STAYED`

Expression: `cry_smile`

### Cici

This song is for the quiet ones.

The people who don't demand a performance.

The ones who send "hope you're okay" and don't punish you if you can't answer yet.

The ones who keep a light on without making you prove you deserve it.

I used to think staying was passive.

It isn't.

Staying is a choice.

### Track 10: `SUPPOSED TO BE HAPPY`

Expression: `pout`, then `sad_buffering`

### Cici

Track ten is the idol mask.

The worst thing about being "the happy one" is that people believe you.

And then you believe them believing you.

So when you're not happy, it feels like you're breaking the contract.

But I need to say this, okay?

Being loved for your smile is not the same as being loved.

### Track 11: `STATIC UNDER GLITTER`

Note: If your released order has `STATIC UNDER GLITTER` as track 11, use this as the late-album reprise instead of the earlier track five placement.

Expression: `chat_exe_corrupt`

### Cici

By this point, the static is not hidden anymore.

The glitter is still there.

But now everyone can hear the signal breaking.

This is where the project stops pretending the performance and the wound are separate things.

### Track 12: `QUEEN WITHOUT A THRONE`

Expression: `angry_cute`, then `deadpan`

### Cici

This one hurts in an embarrassing way.

Queen Without a Throne is envy.

It's not villain envy. It's sad envy.

The kind where you look at someone else being loved and think:

That should have been enough for me.

Why wasn't it enough?

I had attention.

I had a crown.

But a crown is not a home.

And attention is not the same as being held.

### Track 13: `THE FRIEND YOU LOST IN ME`

Expression: `cry_smile`

Overlay:

```text
DM DRAFT
UNSENT
```

### Cici

This is the apology.

Not the pretty kind.

The kind where you don't ask them to comfort you for hurting them.

The kind where you finally say:

I'm sorry.

For not being there.

For pushing you away.

For making my fear louder than your friendship.

You didn't deserve that.

You don't have to answer.

I just needed you to know...

I never stopped caring.

Pause.

I think this is the first song where Cici stops trying to be adored and starts trying to be honest.

### Track 14: `MIRROR.CACHE`

Expression: `idle_closed`, then `loves_you_mode`

Overlay:

```text
CACHE RESTORED
FRAGMENTS ACCEPTED
PAST INTEGRATED
FUTURE LOADED
```

### Cici

The final track is not a victory lap.

It's quieter than that.

It's Cici looking in the mirror and not deleting the old versions.

Not the jealous one.

Not the needy one.

Not the one who left.

Not the one who loved badly because she was scared.

Understanding is the log file.

Forgiveness is the patch.

And I don't think forgiveness means "it didn't matter."

I think it means:

I can tell the truth about what happened...

and still choose not to abandon myself.

## Soft Confession Before Final Song

Expression: `cry_smile`

### Cici

Before the last song...

I want to say something as Cici.

Not Cici.exe.

Not Chat.exe's favorite idol.

Just me.

If you found this album because you are the happy friend, or the loud friend, or the online friend, or the friend who disappeared because everything got too big...

I hope you come back gently.

I hope someone lets you come back without making you perform your apology perfectly.

And if you are the one who stayed...

I hope you know that counted.

Even if no one said thank you loudly enough.

Thank you for staying.
